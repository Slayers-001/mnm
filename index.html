WINTER

The sky is a bruised purple, heavy with the weight of a thousand storms. As the first notes of the concerto pierce the silence, the atmosphere shifts— particles of frost begin to dance in the neon glow of the nexus. In this frozen digital wasteland, the music is the only thing that feels warm.

Vivaldi’s strings pull at the very fabric of the permafrost, weaving a narrative of sharp winds and crystalline beauty. Welcome to the solstice.
